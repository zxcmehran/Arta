You can easily embed external resources into your posts using oEmbed system. Arta supports oEmbed system natively. To embed an external resource, just put resource URL between {embed} and {/embed} tags. Example:

{embed}http://www.viddler.com/explore/cdevroe/videos/424/{/embed}

Everything is done. The video will be embedded inside your post.
You can use maxwidth and maxheight parameters which define maximum dimensions of embedded content. Example:

{embed maxheight=500 maxwidth=450}http://flickr.com/photos/bees/2362225867/{/embed}

The embedded content will obey your max dimensions.

Note: The Plug-in content::oembed_handler should be active.
